# 03. Operator Personas & Role-Based Experience

## Defined Personas
1. **Executive**: Focuses on Health Indexes, Certification Status, and broad Qualification readiness.
2. **Platform Operator**: Focuses on Observability, Benchmarking, and Resource Budgets.
3. **Platform Administrator**: Manages Governance, RBAC, and Infrastructure configuration.
4. **AI Engineer**: Oversees Platform Intelligence, Model integration, and Participant lifecycle.
5. **Knowledge Engineer**: Manages Knowledge Graphs and memory stores.
6. **Workflow Designer**: Creates DAGs and orchestrates multi-agent flows.
7. **Prompt Engineer**: Iterates on system prompts and capability schemas.
8. **Developer**: Integrates MCP capabilities and tests UEP boundaries.
9. **Architect**: Explores Digital Twins and defines Constitutional invariants.
10. **Security Administrator**: Monitors Policy Service (PPS) logs and Zero-Trust boundaries.

## Role-Based Experience Model
The OXA enforces adaptive experiences through NavigationService role filtering:
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Executives see aggregate KPI dials; Operators see real-time log tails and D3 topology graphs.
- **Visible Navigation**: Automatically pruned based on the user's active oles array in the IExecutionContext.
- **Default Landing Pages**: Architects land on the Architecture Explorer; Administrators land on Governance.
